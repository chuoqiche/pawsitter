Who is PawSitter

PawSitter is the right place for all things adorable such as your pets.Even if you're a beginner pet parent or highly experienced caregiver, we offer the best of the best services and products that will leave you stunned at the experience your pets are gonna have here! Our goal is to maintain your precious companions' mental and physical health while letting them have a fun and safe environment to play in. We only use non pesticides and organic food to feed your pets, premium toys and assessories for your pets to enjoy in order to deliver them the best experience they could have


Our Mission

  • Providing love, care, and happiness for pets while ensuring their safety and well-being
  • Delivering peace of mind to pet parents through compassionate and reliable pet care.
  • Creating joyful experiences for pets in a safe and nurturing environment.
  • Transforming time away from home into moments of comfort and fun for your pets.
  • Prioritizing every pet's happiness, health, and playful spirit with thoughtful care.
  • Building a world where pets feel loved and their owners feel reassured.









We began as a small group of animal lovers with a big dream. Volunteering at shelters and helping stray pets brought us closer to the joy and challenges of caring for animals. Each story of a lonely dog finding comfort or a scared cat finally purring in safety inspired us to do more.
What started as a passion soon became our mission. After countless hours spent comforting abandoned pets, fostering furry friends, and supporting overwhelmed pet owners, we realized we wanted to create a space where all pets could feel loved and cared for, even when their families couldn't be there.
