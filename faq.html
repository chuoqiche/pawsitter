PawSitter Day Care

What do our pets get to do at the DayCare

We provide your fellow companions with regular exercise activity. They get to socialize with other pets and people at the DayCare. They are fed one meal and treats during their stay at PawSitter DayCare. The food that we feed them is organic and natural, therefore there are no harmful chemicals or pesticides added in their food.

What are the benefits of joining PawSitter DayCare?

Your pets get to maintain a healthy weight. Increased happiness. Relief of separation anxiety and boredom. They become less hostile towards other people due to the number of times they get to interact with different people and even other pets.

What kind of environment will my pets play in?

Our DayCare consists of two play areas, which are Indoor and Outdoor play areas.

Why should I choose PawSitter DayCare instead of other shops?

PawSitter offers a wide range of activities for your pets to enjoy depending on their mood and personality. For adventurous and playful pets, they are able to have as much fun during their time here with fun toys and accessories. On the other hand, for pets that are less active and older, our DayCare facility provides comfy rest beds and mattresses for them to sleep and take a nap. Furthermore, our caregivers are certified and professionally trained to take good care of your precious pets. Most importantly, we put an overall importance on your pet’s mental and physical health. When they are playing with each other, accidents might happen. By that, if they are hurt in any way, we take full responsibility and accountability to make sure your pets are treated properly without any additional or hidden fees.

Do I need to make a reservation before joining?

Yes, you can make a reservation by clicking the “make a reservation button” below the page or click here to be redirected to it.

Are we able to take a tour before registration?

Of course! You can definitely take a look around before joining our program. We do highly respect our customers' opinions and will take note of any issues they have for our facilities, etc.

Are there any installment plans?

Yes, we do, but there are requirements to fulfill before you're able to do so
>You must be a member of PawSitter DayCare.
>Installment plans are not available for one-time visits or any one-time services.
>You must sign an agreement form with our service center before you are able to fully use any installment plans of your choice.
>Lower income households below RM2500 are eligible for installment plans immediately without any further requirements.

Are there any additional fees when joining PawSitter DayCare?

All our fees are inclusive when you make your first payment, therefore there are no additional or hidden fees.
